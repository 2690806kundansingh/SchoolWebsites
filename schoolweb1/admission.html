Admissions Process

Our admissions process is designed to help us get to know your family and ensure Greenfield is the right fit for your child. Below is our step-by-step admissions process:

Step 1: Inquiry

Submit our online inquiry form or contact our admissions office to schedule a tour.

Step 2: Application

Complete our online application and submit required documents.

Step 3: Assessment

Students complete grade-appropriate assessments (Grades 1-12).

Step 4: Family Interview

Meet with our admissions team to discuss your child's needs and our programs.

Step 5: Decision

Receive admissions decision within 2 weeks of completing all steps.

Frequently Asked Questions

When is the application deadline?

We have rolling admissions, but priority consideration is given to applications received by March 1 for the following academic year. Late applications are accepted based on availability.

Do you offer financial aid?

Yes, we offer need-based financial aid to qualified families. The financial aid application process runs concurrently with the admissions process. Approximately 25% of our students receive some form of financial assistance.

What are your school hours?

Our regular school hours are 8:00 AM to 3:00 PM. Extended care is available from 7:30 AM to 8:00 AM and from 3:00 PM to 6:00 PM for an additional fee.

Do you accept mid-year transfers?

Yes, we accept mid-year transfers if space is available in the appropriate grade level. The admissions process is the same, though we may expedite the timeline for mid-year applicants.

What is your student-to-teacher ratio?

Our average class size is 18 students with a student-to-teacher ratio of 8:1. In lower grades, we maintain smaller class sizes with additional teaching assistants.
